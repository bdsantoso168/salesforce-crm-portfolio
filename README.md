## Salesforce CRM Transformation Case Study

**Sequencing matters more than software.**

A full business analysis and Salesforce solution design for a B2B precision manufacturer carrying three revenue problems at once: quoting delays, slow partner onboarding, and reactive field service.

**Live site:** https://bdsantoso168.github.io/salesforce-crm-portfolio/

## What this covers

- **The challenge**: three competing executive priorities, revenue velocity, fixed budget, and SAP integration sequencing, layered on top of a CRM rollout that had already failed once
- **The approach**: As-Is to To-Be process redesign across the Lead-to-Quote cycle
- **The roadmap**: a phased delivery sequence, Sales Cloud with CPQ, Service Cloud, and Experience Cloud delivered first
- **Solution architecture**: how the three clouds share one Salesforce core and one source of truth
- **Deliverables**: the full solution deck and the boardroom-cut executive deck, both linked below
